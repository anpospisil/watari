What We Do

Our Mission is to facilitate meaningful change and provide a bridge to healthier possibilities through innovative, community-driven, and supportive programming.

Our Philosophy

We encourage an exploration and deeper understanding of self and community.

Objectives

To empower people, we support, through several individual and community-minded approaches.

Our Impact

Since its inception in 1986, Watari Counselling & Support Services Society has impacted the lives of thousands of at-risk children, youth, their families and communities in the Downtown Eastside and the Greater Vancouver/Richmond areas.

Help Us Create Change

We rely on the help and support of our community to make a difference in the lives of the people we encounter in our work. Donations are used to improve services and remove barriers for those seeking help through our Youth, Adult, and Community Counselling Programs!

As a social-profit organization, Watari is very thankful and acknowledges the generous contributions of funding partners, in-kind donors, and members of the community. These partners continue to help make our services and programs a success.

Since its inception in 1986, Watari Counselling & Support Services Society has impacted the lives of thousands of at-risk children, youth, and their families.

Watari’s ‘Stop Think Assess & Respond‘ program is presented in classrooms all around Greater Vancouver for grades 4 to 7, offering a ‘just say KNOW’ approach to drugs, safety, compassion, and community.
